Objective of this documents:
    Describe the specifications related to the collected and converted genomic data.

Overview:
    The created .py script to extract and convert genomic data to npubs can be applied in the entirely NCBI related databases.
    Although, the first created nanopub dataset will concentrate on some specific instances to test all the benefits of providing such data.
    The final dataset will have data related to:
        - Neglected tropical diseases (Eukaryotes only).
            - Following the "Ending the neglect to attain the Sustainable Development Goals: A road map for neglected tropical diseases 2021–2030".
            - https://www.who.int/publications/i/item/9789240010352
        - Random instances of GBAD database.
        - Critical Organisms.
            - Organism that have a plenty of different related strains.
